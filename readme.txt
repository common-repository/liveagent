= LiveAgent Core Features =
LiveAgent's core features include:

**Universal inbox**
LiveAgent's [universal inbox](https://www.liveagent.com/features/universal-inbox/#wordpress) is a smarter version of your email inbox. Emails, chats, calls, social media messages, forum posts and suggestions -- they're all automatically pushed into your LiveAgent account in the form of a ticket.

**Hybrid ticketing system**
A hybrid ticket stream contains a [complete stream](https://www.liveagent.com/features/hybrid-ticket-stream/#wordpress) of chat messages, emails, phone calls, or messages from other communication channels about the same incident or issue reported by a customer. Each ticket has got a unique reference number, which helps you quickly locate, add to, or communicate the status of the user's issue or request.

**Live chat:**
Currently, LiveAgent boasts [the fastest chat widget on the market](https://www.debugbear.com/blog/chat-widget-site-performance) that displays in just 2.5 seconds. It enables you to chat with your customers in real-time and allows you to make use of pre-chat forms and offline forms.

**Call center:**
Build a virtual [call center](https://www.liveagent.com/call-center-software/#wordpress) as a part of your multi-channel help desk solution. LiveAgent will centralize phone calls from your website or landline into your LiveAgent dashboard.

**Customer Portal**
The [customer portal](https:/www.liveagent.com/features/customer-portal/#wordpress) stores your knowledge base articles, forum posts, and feedback from your customers. LiveAgent's customer portals are fully customizable, so you can make them match your business style.

**Reporting:**
Get an overview of your customer support. See who is interacting with your company and monitor the service they're receiving. See usage, statistics, evaluate performance reports, and learn from customer satisfaction ratings.

**Mobile Apps:**
LiveAgent offers both iOS and Android apps so you can provide excellent customer service on the go.

**Customer service:**
24 Hour customer service and white-glove setup

== Installation ==
No HTML knowledge is required to set up this plugin. Integration takes less than 3 minutes.
You can also visit our <a href='http://support.qualityunit.com/140502-Overview' target='_blank' title='Installation guide'>step-by-step guide</a> that will help you install the plugin.

Here is the basic guide:
1. While logged in your WordPress admin panel navigate to Plugins> Add new 
2. Find LiveAgent plugin, install and activate it
3. Create a free account or connect to an existing LiveAgent account
4. Navigate to menu LiveAgent. Choose the button you'd like to have online on your website and click Save.
5. Navigate to the LiveAgent admin panel and start answering chats and emails from your website visitors.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= What is LiveAgent? =
LiveAgent is a [customer support software](https://www.liveagent.com/#wordpress). The LiveAgent WordPress plugin automatically adds a LiveAgent chat widget to your WordPress website.

= Is this plugin available in my language? =
From version 3.0.0 the plugin supports translations to any language that WordPress supports. If you have time and want to help us improve our plugin feel free to [Make your own translation](http://support.qualityunit.com/634703-Making-custom-translation) and send it to us. We will add it to the next plugin release.

= Is LiveAgent free? How much does it cost? =
LiveAgent offers four different tier packages. The lowest tier package is forever free but does have certain limitations. The free version offers free live chat, free ticketing system, free call center, free knowledge base, and free unlimited agent seats. The paid packages vary in price starting from $15/month up to $39/month.

= Can I customize the chat widget? =
LiveAgent's chat widgets are fully customizable.

= Is the history of conversations saved? =
LiveAgent's free version has a 7-day search history, but the paid plans offer an unlimited search history.

= Is there a mobile application? =
LiveAgent has both iOS and Android mobile apps available.

= Can I use LiveAgent with multiple sites? =
Yes, LiveAgent can be used with multiple sites.

= Do you offer a free trial? =
LiveAgent offers a [free 14-day trial](https://www.liveagent.com/trial/#wordpress) with no credit card required.

= Can I use live chat service on any website? =
Yes, LiveAgent's live chat is available on all sites where you are allowed to add custom code.

= How does the live chat app work after hours? =
If a customer wants to chat with you after your business hours, their chat will be sent to you in the form of a ticket.

= Can I live chat with customers on my mobile app? =
Yes, LiveAgent mobile apps do support live chat.

= Do you have any testimonials? =
Yes, feel free to check out what our customers are saying about us. LiveAgent has collected more than 2800 reviews on various software marketplaces like [Capterra](https://www.capterra.com/p/102188/LiveAgent/reviews/), [Trustpilot](https://www.trustpilot.com/review/ladesk.com), [GetApp](https://www.getapp.com/customer-service-support-software/a/liveagent/reviews/), [G2](https://www.g2.com/products/liveagent/reviews#reviews) and [Software Advice](https://www.softwareadvice.com/live-chat/liveagent-profile/reviews/).
